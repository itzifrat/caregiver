FAQs for finding senior care in Houston, TX

In 2024, how much does senior care cost near me in Houston, TX?

The average rate for hiring a senior care provider in Houston, TX on Care.com is $17.15 per hour as of September 2024. This rate will vary depending on the level of care needed (medical/non-medical or overnight care), the provider's certifications, years of experience, and total amount of hours required per day/week.

What types of senior care are available near me in 2024 in Houston, TX?

You can find a variety of in-home senior care options in Houston, TX on Care.com. Some of these options include having a companion to support your loved one with daily activities such as getting around the house, helping with meal prep, light housekeeping or even dressing/bathing. You also have the option to find a nursing assistant if medical care is required. There are also caregivers who can provide senior transportation to run errands or drive to doctor appointments. For seniors that require highly monitored care or cannot remain in their own homes, you'll find other options in Houston such as assisted living facilities or nursing homes.
