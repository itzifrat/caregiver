FAQs for finding dog daycare near you

What is dog day care?

Dog day care is a service that provides care for dogs when their owners cant look after them. At a dog daycare, dogs can play with other dogs under the supervision of trained staff and can get some much needed exercise. Some dog day care centers also offer helpful services like training and grooming.

How much does dog day care cost?

Where you live, the care services you require, how many dogs you have, and your chosen dog day care center can all impact dog day care costs. That being said, the average hourly rate for dog day care nationwide on Care.com is $0.00 as of September, 2024. You can review Care.com profiles to learn more about dog day care prices near you.

Are dog day cares safe?

Dog day care can provide a safe and stimulating environment for your dog when you cant home. Its important to find the right fit for your dog. For example, if you have a very small dog or are seeking puppy daycare options, you will want to make sure your dog day care has play groups for different sized and aged dogs so your little pooch doesnt get too overwhelmed. If your dog really benefits from outdoor time, you will want to look for a dog day care with an outdoor play area to make sure theyre in the right environment. You can also read reviews of different dog day care centers on Care.com to learn more about which dog day cares may be the best fit.

What will my dog do at dog day care?

The activities that your dog will participate in at dog day care what activities the day care center supports and their personalities. Some dog day care centers offer activities like obedience training, agility courses, and playgroups. Other dog day care centers may have a more relaxed vibe where dogs have access to free play.

How often should I take my dog to dog day care?

How often you should take your dog to dog day care will depend on your schedule, budget, and the needs of your dog. Some people take their dogs to dog day care on a daily or weekly basis, while others only use the service occasionally when they need someone to look after their dog for a few hours or a full day (like when they have to attend a special event or have a long work day). You can also take your dog to a dog boarding kennel if you need longer term care while on vacation.
