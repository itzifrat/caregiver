What impacts pay rates

Several factors can impact what you pay a housekeeper. First calculate your basic hourly pay rate. Then use the calculator above to adjust it to your specific house cleaning needs.

Geographical location

  • Major metropolitan areas tend to have higher rates than rural areas.
  • Suburbs can also have higher rates than rural areas, especially if they serve a large commuter population and are near a major city.

Hours of work

  • Do you need a live-in housekeeper, part-time housekeeper or just occasional cleaning?
  • Enter the number of hours you expect the housekeeper to work and then adjust the rate to your individual situation.

Additional services

  • Asking your housekeeper to help with things like deep cleaning or doing the laundry may increase the cost. Be sure to speak with your housekeeper about the things that are included in your rate so that both of you know what the expectations are.
  • In addition, be sure to specify if you will require your housekeeper to bring cleaning equipment & cleaning supplies as this can also impact the final rate.
